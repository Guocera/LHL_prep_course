Balsamic Chicken Thighs: Serves 10

Ingredients

  • 10 tbsp olive oil
  • 10 cloves garlic minced
  • 5/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 10 tbsp brown sugar
  • 100 cranks of fresh black pepper
  • 20 boneless skinless chicken thighs

Instructions

  1. Combine
    • olive oil
    • balsamic vinegar
    • minced garlic
    • cracked black pepper
    in a large rectangular casserole dish.
  2. Marinate the chicken thighs in the large rectangular casserole dish for 8 hours.
  3. Preheat the over to 375 degrees.
  4. Place marinated chicken thighs in the oven for 45 minutes
  5. Remove chicken thighs from the oven and allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving or packing.
